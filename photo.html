A Breif Overview

This project is to design a webpage for a local freelance photographer and videographer to show their work. The project will consist of identifying the client's needs for the website and what they will want for the users. The research will be to check out local competitor sites. After that, I will be creating the UI for the website based on my research and the goals of the client.

My Role

I will be the sole designer on these projects and will be creating the layout of this new website. As well as conducting research and prototyping the final design. I will be designing possible logos that the client could use for there branding.

Goals

This website is for a small business owner that wants to have a branded webpage to get more clients and have a place to direct people to view their work online. This site is to be used as an alternative to just using social media sites to show their work..

The client needs a domain on the internet to showcase their body of work to potential clients.

There will also need to be a place on the site that will allow people to contact them as well as placing links to their social sites.

Reqiuremants and constraints

  • A landing page with a hero image showcasing some of there photography
  • A Gallery page with there photography
  • A place for clients to contact them
  • A logo for there website and products
  • there site needs to be comleted before Jan 1, 2020
  • A place for them to showcase there reel

Branding and logo design

I expramented with some possible logo designs that might be used.

Gallery page design

One of the most important requirements for this project was to create a page where the client can display there body of work. This was one of the main facets of the project and needed to be done with a tasteful visual design. I came up with this layout after researching similar websites.

Contact form

A form was needed to contact the photographer. It was important for the user to have a place to be able to contact the photographer and schedule photoshoots.

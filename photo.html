A Brief Overview

A local freelance photographer and videographer wants to get more traffic to their business and have a place to show their work online. They need a website to serve as their place to show and brand their work, and their business, as well as provide a way for clients to contact them. This project will consist of designing a functional web page for a small business that meets the needs of the client. I will also be designing a logo that they might use on their website, products that they might like to sell in the future as well as on business cards.

My Role

I will be the solo designer and developer on this project. I will start by designing a logo for the client. Next I will design the UI for their website. I will be creating low-fi wireframes and final mockups of the UI of their website, as well as prototyping and developing the website later on.

Goals

Previously my client has been using social media to promote their work. However this is not ideal because not everyone uses the same social media platforms. They need a webpage to direct clients to so that everyone will be able to see their work. The overall goal is to be able to promote their business to everyone and display their work to the public.

The client needs a domain on the internet to showcase their body of work to potential clients.

There will also need to be a place on the site that will allow people to contact them.

The website should have links to their social media.

Requirements and Constraints

  • A landing page with a hero image showcasing a slideshow of their work
  • A Gallery page with their photography
  • A place for clients to contact them
  • A logo for their website and products
  • A place for them to showcase there reel
  • Their site needs to be completed before Jan 1, 2020

Branding and Logo Design

One of the requirements was to design a logo that the client could use for the website, business cards or any other needs that the client might have. I experimented with some possible logo designs that might be used.

Visual Exploration

Sketches and lo-fi wireframes were created to explore possibilities for the layout of the site. I will keep in mind what the client needs their users to get from the website Users need to be able to view their work and contact them through the website. Another important detail to add to the website will be links to their social media sites so people can get to their businesses facebook and instagram accounts.

Final UI

The Gallery page was one of the most important aspects of the site. I wanted the visual layout of the site to be pleasing to look at and allow the user to click on and view the images close up as well as on a grid.

I designed a contact form for the user to get intouch with the client.

Solution

If you recall, in the beginning of our project we were given the problem of a client wanting to be able to reach out to more customers and clients. They needed to be able to expand their business and have a gallery to show their work online. I designed a functional UI to give their work and their business a home on the internet and allow them to grow past solely using social media platforms to showcase their work. This will allow the client to get more people's attention as well as having a more professional feel to their work and business.

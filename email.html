A Breif Overview

Everyone uses an email platform to communicate and send each other information whether that is for personal or professional use. Students as well as businesses use if as a primary means of communication and passing of information.

My Role

I will be taking a look at similar sites and ideatifying some of the problems that they have for the users. I will be wireframeing and creating high fidilty mockups of the new site. As well as prototyping the new email platform.

The main problem and goal

It is important for a user to be able to find their messages quickly and easily,saving important mail as well as sending their own emails to people. Many of the current email platforms contain tools that the average user doesn't need when all they need to do is see their messages and send outgoing mail to businesses, school and personal acquaintances. This project will consist of designing an email homepage that has the most important tools and information where people can access easily and do away with some of the visual clutter.

Reqiuremants for this project

  • Make the most used functions the post promoent
  • Create a slide out manu for all the tools
  • Make sure the search bar is clear and easy to find
  • make things look sleek and modern

Ideation

After the research is done and the user needs have been identified as well as stating the main aspects of the site that need to be incorporated and updated from other sights that have been investigated the visual design now needs to be explored. Wireframes were created to iterate through the initial stages of the design. Exploring where and how the information will be laid out of the design of this new webpage. The design was carried out through both mobile and desktop layouts

As stated above some important things that needed to be addressed in the site was the need for updating the look to be sleek and modern. On my exploration through similar sites many of the components were laid out similarly, and I believed that it was important to keep a familiar design throughout the new pages. Because it will be easier for new users to transfer over across platforms.

Solution

We started out needing to create a new design for an email platform that was sampled for the average user.

Overview

MyFitnessApp was an application designed to add users in maintaning and increasing There fitness.

My Role

My role is this project is to conduct user interviews to gather insights into the habits and needs of those that my be intrested in using this application. I then take the infomation I have gathered to the design phase. Creating a entirely new application based on the features that where important to the people that I interview.

Understand

The problem

Its increasingly importment to stay motivated about your fitness and want to improve it. With this app I aim to increase the users enjoyment and motivation around exersize. As well as allowing them to set goals and improve their fitness.

  • Disign a feature that will allow users to set and change goals
  • Create features that will show the users progress over the coarse of days/weeks/months
  • Make the features pleasing and easy to understand

The Solution

Athiletes/runners/hikers need an app that will allow them to improve their fitness habits and motivate them to set their own goals for improvement. they need and application design so thay can keep track and stay with their fitness plan as will as let them view their improvements over the course of time.

Research/Analyze

User Interviews

During the interview and research I was able to access the types of people that will be useing the fitness app as well as their needs of the new app I was able to gather information as to way and how they will use the app and for what purpose. I was importent to astablish the probloms that this app needed to solve.

User insights

  • Users will need an app that is simple and easy to understand and use and that conveys the necessary data in a easy to read way.
  • Users of this fitness app like to be able to track their progress and improvment over day/weeks/months.
  • Fitness users Competitive and want to improve their workout over time.

Personas

From the imformation gathered from the interviews I was able to determine a few diffrent types of people who may be using the app. I have created diffrent personas for those poeple. One is an expreinced fitness coach look to give his clients a tool to modivate them and allow them to track their progress. One is a women who wants to get back into fitness and needs to plan workout sessions. The last is a teenager needed a tool to help him improve in competition is his high school track team.

I then choose one persona to create a scenario for the applications use.
